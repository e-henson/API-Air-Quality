    pollut.or uses the World Air Quality Index (WAQI) API to pull in data allowing users to search the air quality for locations all around the world!

    Index of indicators:

    Air Quality Index: 0 - 50

    Good: air quality poses little to no risk

    Air Quality Index: 51 - 100

    Moderate: air quality is acceptable, but some pollutants might be of moderate health concern for sensitive groups

    Air Quality Index: 101 - 150

    Unhealthy for sensitive groups: general public not likely to be affected, but members of sensitive groups are likely to experience health effects

    Air Quality Index: 151 - 200

    Unhealthy: everyone may begin to experience health effects

    Air Quality Index: 201 - 300

    Very unhealthy: everyone may begin to experience serious health effects

    Air Quality Index: 301 - 500

    Hazardous: the entire population is likely to be affected
